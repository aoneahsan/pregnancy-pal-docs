# Quiet hours

> **Quiet hours** in PregnancyPal are an overnight window — a start time, an end time, and an enable toggle — during which the app's wellness reminders are suppressed so they do not disturb your sleep.

Rest matters in pregnancy, and a buzzing phone at 2 a.m. helps no one. Quiet hours let you draw a line around your sleep: pick when your quiet window starts and ends, switch it on, and PregnancyPal holds back its wellness reminders during that time. You get the helpful nudges during the day and an undisturbed night.

Quiet hours govern the app's own reminders. They are part of how PregnancyPal tries to be a calm companion rather than a source of stress.

## What you can do with quiet hours

- **Set a start time** for your quiet window, in 24-hour `HH:mm` format.
- **Set an end time** for your quiet window, in `HH:mm` format.
- **Enable or disable** quiet hours with a single toggle.
- **Protect overnight sleep** by suppressing wellness reminders during the window you chose.
- **Change the window any time** as your sleep schedule shifts through pregnancy.

## How to set quiet hours

1. **Open your preferences**: Profile → Preferences (`/profile/preferences`).
2. **Find the quiet hours controls** in the notifications area.
3. **Enter a start time** for your quiet window in `HH:mm` (for example, `22:00`).
4. **Enter an end time** for your quiet window in `HH:mm` (for example, `07:00`).
5. **Turn on the enable toggle** so quiet hours take effect.
6. **Confirm your daytime reminders still work** by reviewing your [notification preferences](./preferences) — only the overnight window is silenced.

## Examples

- **A standard night's sleep** — You set quiet hours from `22:00` to `07:00` and enable them. Wellness reminders that would normally arrive overnight are held back, so your night stays calm. During the day they flow as usual.
- **An early riser** — You wake at 5 a.m., so you set the window to `21:30`–`05:00`. Your reminders stop well before bedtime and resume right when your day begins.
- **Temporarily off** — You are travelling and want to keep an eye on reminders around the clock, so you switch the enable toggle off. Quiet hours stop applying until you turn the toggle back on, with your start and end times still saved for later.

## Tips

- **Match the window to your real sleep, not the clock you wish you kept.** If you usually fall asleep at 11 p.m., start quiet hours a little earlier so wind-down time is protected too.
- **Use the 24-hour format.** Times are entered as `HH:mm`, so `10 p.m.` is `22:00` and `7 a.m.` is `07:00`.
- **Adjust it as pregnancy changes your sleep.** Many people sleep differently across trimesters — revisit the window whenever your rhythm shifts.
- **Pair it with chosen categories.** Quiet hours decide *when* reminders are silenced; your [notification preferences](./preferences) decide *which* reminders exist in the first place.
- **Remember it is for the app's reminders.** Quiet hours shape PregnancyPal's wellness nudges — they are not a system-wide do-not-disturb for your whole phone.

## Frequently asked questions

### What exactly do quiet hours suppress?

They suppress PregnancyPal's wellness reminders during the start-to-end window you set, while the enable toggle is on. It is the app's way of not disturbing your sleep.

### How do I enter the times?

As a start time and an end time in `HH:mm` (24-hour) format — for example `22:00` to `07:00`. Then switch on the enable toggle for the window to take effect.

### Do quiet hours mute my entire phone?

No. Quiet hours govern PregnancyPal's own reminders, not your device's system-wide notifications. For a full do-not-disturb across all apps, use your phone's built-in setting.

### Will I miss important reminders overnight?

Wellness reminders during the window are held back by design. PregnancyPal's reminders are non-urgent tips and nudges, so holding them until morning is intentional — none of them is time-critical or medical.

### Can I turn quiet hours off without losing my times?

Yes. Switching the enable toggle off stops quiet hours from applying, while your saved start and end times remain ready for when you turn it back on.

### Does this affect daytime tips and reminders?

No. Only the overnight window you set is affected. Outside that window, the categories you enabled in [notification preferences](./preferences) reach you normally.

## What this feature does NOT do

- **Does not silence your whole device.** Quiet hours apply to PregnancyPal's wellness reminders only — not to calls, messages, or other apps. Use your phone's system do-not-disturb for that.
- **Does not hold back anything urgent or medical.** PregnancyPal does not send medical alerts at all, so nothing time-critical is ever being delayed by quiet hours.
- **Does not detect when you are actually asleep.** It simply follows the start and end times you entered; it does not sense your sleep.
- **Does not change which reminders exist.** It only affects timing — your category choices live in [notification preferences](./preferences).

**Quiet hours manage convenience reminders so you can rest — they are not a medical or safety feature and are not medical advice. If you have a health concern at any hour, contact your healthcare provider or emergency services directly rather than waiting for an app.**
